Subject: New Feature: Email Performance Attendees

Hi Team,

We've added a new feature that makes it easy to send custom messages to all ticket holders for a specific performance.

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Go to any production page in the admin interface
2. Click "Email Attendees" next to the performance
3. Write your message (supports bold, italic, bullets, etc. using Markdown)
4. Click send - the system automatically emails everyone with tickets

WHEN TO USE IT:

• Weather-related updates
• Show Cancellations
• Modifications to existing show
• Emergency Parking or arrival information (weather?)
• Any last-minute, performance-specific updates

WHAT THEY'LL SEE:

Recipients get a clean email with:
- Personal greeting ("Hi [First Name],")
- Your custom message
- Theater branding and contact info

No order details or ticket information - just your message.

The system automatically filters to only send to confirmed orders in Held, Processed or Fulfilled states with valid email addresses, and shows you the recipient count before you send.
